Course Title : Introduction to Physics
This course provides a structured approach to mastering the fundamentals of Physics. The content includes:
  • Classical Mechanics: Explore the basic principles of motion, forces, energy, and momentum in the physical world.
  • Electromagnetism: Learn about electric fields, magnetic fields, and the relationship between electricity and magnetism.
  • Thermodynamics: Understand the laws of heat, energy transfer, and the principles governing temperature and work.
  • Quantum Physics: Delve into the world of subatomic particles, wave-particle duality, and the principles of quantum mechanics.
By the end of this course, you will have a solid foundation in physics, with the knowledge to apply scientific principles to real-world problems.
